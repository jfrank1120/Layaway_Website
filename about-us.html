From Humble Beginnings

Layaway began as a group of friends who started an iMessage group chat freshman year of high school in 2012. However, almost 10 years later that group of 4 friends (now known as the founding fathers) has transformed the groupchat into one of a kind. Spawning several offchute chats such as the Wall Street investment chat, the Sports Betting Chat, the Fantasy Sports Chat, and many more. The groupchat has become a home to a group of friends who have experienced so much together during these times. From figuring out high school, to going off to college, to graduating, to a global pandemic, Layaway has stayed strong through it all no matter the circumstances and hopes to continue that trend into the future.
